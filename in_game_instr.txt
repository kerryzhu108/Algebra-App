This program is designed for the practice and visualization of what happens to numbers
when they are being moved around in an algebraic equation. (Still very buggy)

Random Equation: Generates a random equation with an unknown x variable to solve.
Random Length: Generates an even more random equation but of desired length.
Custom Equation: Type a custom equation to generate.

Numbers can be dragged around via clicking.
Groups of numbers may be simplified by clicking the select box and highlighting the numbers.
Press Z to undo action, R to redo action.
Answer button reveals final answer.
